Overview

Tumblr is a microblogging app that allows users to post and browse a variety of multimedia content, including photos, videos, text and more. Users can connect with others over shared interests by liking, re-blogging, commenting or sharing content. Tumblr is interested in learning how users find and form communities on the platform. Specifically, they want to understand how users navigate the website to find users and content they care about. Tumblr is also interested in how users come to identify themselves as belonging to a community.

Target Demogrpahic

The stakeholder interview clarified the project scope and research areas. Tumblr’s previous user demographic studies found that the majority of primary users are between 14-21 years old. They are also interested in learning more about users who care about art and social justice, among other interests.

Research Questions

Community Discovery​: How do people find and follow the things they care about?

Community Engagement​: What does it take to feel like you're part of a community onTumblr?

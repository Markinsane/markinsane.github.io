Introduction

What is ProQuest Dialog for Pharma?

Currently, pharmaceutical companies rely on ProQuest Dialog to monitor scientific literature for advances and news on drugs and therapies through alerts.They can then use ProQuest Pi2's Drug Safety Triager and related services to automate the review, categorization, and indexing of alert results.These results then flow into ProQuest Pi2’s PinPoint product — a customized repository that offers market-leading search and content management functionality and delivers necessary regulatory reports.The combination of ProQuest Dialog and ProQuest Pi2 significantly improves and simplifies drug safety workflows in the industry. However, the two systems remain independent, so ProQuest Dialog will continue to feed alerts to other providers of product literature databases and ProQuest Pi2 will continue to ingest alerts from other aggregators.

MARK HUANG

DESIGN IS THINKING MADE VISUAL

Hi’ I’m Guanchao. I was born with a passion for design and studied in engineering. Now I am an UX/UI designer in pursuit of a master degree in HCI.

UX designers and civil engineers have a lot in common, they all try to alleviate people’s everyday struggle and facilitate people's lives in every possible way.

I believe good design should integrate technology, user's need and business objective into a coherent story.

I have helped ProQuest with their Drug Safety Application. I redesigned many applications to see how much I could push myself creatively.

Majority of my work is mobile app design but my curiosity and obsession spread over experience strategy, complex systems modeling, storycraft, personalization, motion design, and behavioral design.

I’m based in Ann Arbor, but originally come from a really cold city in China.
